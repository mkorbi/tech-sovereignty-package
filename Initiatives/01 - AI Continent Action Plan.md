# AI Continent Action Plan

**Cluster:** Artificial intelligence
**Type:** Commission Communication (COM(2025) 165)
**Published:** 9 April 2025

## Overview

The AI Continent Action Plan sets out the EU's ambition to become a global leader in artificial intelligence — a "leading AI continent." It is the umbrella plan that frames Europe's AI push as both an economic-growth priority and a means of safeguarding democratic values, fundamental rights, and cultural diversity through trustworthy, human-centric AI.

## Five areas of action

The plan organises actions around five key areas:

1. **Computing infrastructure** — building large-scale AI compute (AI Factories and Gigafactories).
2. **Data** — improving access to high-quality data for AI training.
3. **Skills** — developing AI talent and literacy.
4. **Adoption** — accelerating uptake of AI across industry and the public sector.
5. **Simplification** — reducing regulatory friction for AI development and deployment.

## Key deliverables

The **Apply AI Strategy** is the flagship deliverable, driving AI adoption across strategic sectors. The plan also reinforces EU investment in **AI Factories**, and connects to the **Data Union Strategy** and **AI Act**.

## Contribution to tech sovereignty

The plan is the strategic spine of the AI cluster: by building sovereign compute and data capacity and accelerating European adoption, it aims to reduce dependence on non-EU AI providers while keeping AI aligned with EU values.
