# EU Tech Sovereignty — Official Source Documents

Direct links to the actual paper or law document behind each of the 17 initiatives. For EU legislation, the **PDF** link downloads the official Official Journal text; the **EUR-Lex** link is the document landing page (with all language versions and procedure history). For Commission communications/strategies, the **PDF** link downloads the COM document.

*Compiled June 2026. CELEX codes follow the EUR-Lex convention; PDF URL pattern: `eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/PDF/?uri=CELEX:<code>`.*

## Artificial intelligence

### 1. AI Continent Action Plan
- **Document:** Communication COM(2025) 165 final (9 April 2025) + Annex
- **Plan (PDF):** https://ec.europa.eu/newsroom/dae/redirection/document/114523
- **Annex (PDF):** https://ec.europa.eu/newsroom/dae/redirection/document/114522
- **EC page:** https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/library/ai-continent-action-plan

### 2. Apply AI Strategy
- **Document:** Communication COM(2025) 723 final (October 2025) + 3 annexes
- **EUR-Lex:** https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?uri=CELEX:52025DC0723
- **PDF:** https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/PDF/?uri=CELEX:52025DC0723
- **Annexes (PDF):** https://ec.europa.eu/newsroom/dae/redirection/document/120430
- **EC page:** https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/policies/apply-ai

### 3. AI Factories
- *Programme, not a single law.* Established via an amendment to the EuroHPC Regulation; reinforced by the AI Continent Action Plan and the InvestAI initiative.
- **EuroHPC Regulation — Council Regulation (EU) 2021/1173 (PDF):** https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/PDF/?uri=CELEX:32021R1173
- **Amending Regulation (EU) 2024/1732 enabling AI Factories (PDF):** https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/PDF/?uri=CELEX:32024R1732
- **EC page / list of AI Factories:** https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/policies/ai-factories
- **EuroHPC AI Factories portal:** https://eurohpc-ju.europa.eu/ai-factories_en

### 4. AI Act
